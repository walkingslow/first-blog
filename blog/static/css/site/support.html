FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How is Core Design different from Predix UI?

A: Predix UI is only appropriate for Industrial Internet applications that are focused on the operation and management of assets. For most other contexts, including general digital communications, marketing and content-driven websites, and non-Industrial Internet applications, Core Design should be considered.


Q: Will you continue to support prior versions of CDx?

A: No, prior versions of the CDx will no longer be supported.
