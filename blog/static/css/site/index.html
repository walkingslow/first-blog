Building Industrial Internet applications?


The Core Design language deliberately avoids trying to solve design problems within the Industrial Internet space. If you are looking to build an application for industrial assets management and operation, Predix UI is your answer.

Expression of our masterbrand strategy


We are all responsible for ensuring and emphasizing the integrity of GE’s brand in our digital expressions. Core Design is interlocked with Global Brand every step of the way.

Our design language connects GE brand characteristics to specific design principles:

Bold

Explore new interaction paradigms to fearlessly tackle the complex problems. Experiment with groundbreaking design technology. Use visuals that evoke power and scale.
Simple

Create meaningful, future-friendly experiences with solutions that focus on simplicity and immediacy. Go beyond providing data; help users make sense of it. Provide opportunities to confidently change behavior.
Focused

Eliminate anything that doesn't directly reinforce the focus of the experience. Anticipate user intent and context to provide the most focused solutions possible.
Personal

Brand experiences should help make GE's power, imagination, and complexity relatable. Provide opportunities for users to engage with you how, when, and where they want.
